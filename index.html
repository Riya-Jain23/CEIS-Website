Who We Are

Citizens Environment Improvement Society (CEIS) is a registered nonprofit NGO run by passionate citizens of NOIDA and NCR. Since 2002, CEIS has led efforts in Solid Waste Management, Environmental Education, Water Conservation, Herbal Cultivation, and more. With over 75 life members and 17 corporate members, our mission is to create a cleaner, greener NOIDA through sustainable practices and community engagement.

Our Vision

A clean, green and healthy environment across NOIDA driven by citizen participation and sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To protect and improve the environment through education, composting, rainwater harvesting, herbal gardens, and solid waste management.

Our Values

Transparency, community-driven action, ecological responsibility, and grassroots sustainability.

Adobe Green Warrior Project
CEIS trained 1500 students in 20 schools on waste segregation and sustainable waste management. The project supplied bins for degradable, recyclable, hazardous, and e-waste to each school, empowering youth to reduce and manage waste.
CABOL (Campaign Against Burning of Leaves)
CEIS launched CABOL to reduce pollution caused by burning leaves. Through awareness drives, newsletters, and media, CEIS motivated citizens to adopt composting over burning, contributing to cleaner air in NOIDA.
Home-Composters & Sector 54 Eco Park
CEIS developed user-friendly, odor-free home composters for aerobic and vermicomposting. At its Sector 54 Eco Park, CEIS maintains a 3.5-acre site for vermicomposting, herbal gardening, and training in eco-sustainability practices.
